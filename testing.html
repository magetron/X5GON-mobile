Testing Strategy

As a client side application, we test our code thoroughly with a coverage of over 90%+ (if we can count them). Therefore, we are delivering our product to client with great confidence.

Fully-automated:

  • Unit testing
  • Integration testing
  • Performance testing

Semi-automated:

  • Responsive Design testing
  • Compatibility testing
  • User Acceptance testing

Besides pure testing, we also use Jazzy documentation coverage and swiftformat code-linter formatting as part of our quality assurance process.
